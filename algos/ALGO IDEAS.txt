continuation trading
look for earnings that beat expectation while in an uptrend

fibonacci retracement
given the low and the high of a period, normalize to 1 (the low being 1 if uptrending, and vise versa for a downtrend), identify the fibonacci ratios (0.618 (f(n+1)/f(n)), 0.5, 0.382 (f(n+2)/f(n), 0.236 (f(n+3)/f(n)), 0.786 (sqrt(0.618))

weekly trend line break

flat ema
look for a flat area of (20 day) ema, especially after rallying, then look for support/resistance above/below the ema
